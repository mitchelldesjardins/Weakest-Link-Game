THE RULES

  • The game is broken up into three rounds.

  • Use the first two rounds to accumulate money (you cannot lose here).

  • The last round is a sudden death round that will determine if you will walk away with the total money you have earned or label YOU as the “WEAKEST LINK”

Note: the objective of rounds 1 and 2 are to create a chain of consecutive correct answers to climb the “Money Tree” to gain money for your "bank" within a specific time limit.
An incorrect answer throws you to the bottom of the "money tree" and you lose all the money accumulated up to that point. However, YOU the player can say "bank", (press the bank button) prior to a question being asked, then the accumulated money is stored, and the “Money Tree” resets to zero.
You have 4 possible answer choices, there is only one correct answer for each question.
